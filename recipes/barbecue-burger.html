Barbecue Burger

Description:

This Bold Honey-Barbecue Burger recipe combines the sweetness of honey with the smoky flavor of barbecue sauce, creating a mouth-watering burger that's perfect for any barbecue or cookout. Easy to make and bursting with flavor, this burger will quickly become a favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato, and onion slices for garnish

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, honey, barbecue sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix until just combined, being careful not to overwork the meat.
  2. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape each portion into a patty.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Clean and oil the grates to prevent sticking.
  4. Grill the burger patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty and allow it to melt.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill for about 1 minute, or until lightly golden.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing the patties on the buns and adding lettuce, tomato, and onion slices. Add additional barbecue sauce if desired.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your Bold Honey-Barbecue Burger!
